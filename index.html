About the shop

A small space, full of good things.

Valencia Street Vintage began as a parklet pop-up on the sidewalks of the Mission — a few racks, a lot of love, and an eye for the kind of pieces that get better with age.

Today, we live in our own thoughtfully designed storefront on Valencia Street, where every nook is filled with treasures: silk slips that catch the afternoon light, sturdy coats that have already seen a winter or two, denim that's earned its softness, and a $15 rack out front for the joy of an easy yes.

We curate slowly. We price honestly. And we stay open late, because the best finds usually happen after work.
